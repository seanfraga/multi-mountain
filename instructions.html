Multidisciplinarity Mountain is a game.

You are the leader of a multidisciplinary research team.

Your goal: Be the first to reach the summit of Multidisciplinarity Mountain.

All players start at the Base Camp. All players choose a token. The person who most recently received a degree plays first. Play proceeds to the left. Draw one card per turn unless instructed otherwise.

Draw your first card by scanning the QR code for The Foothills. Follow the instructions on the card. To draw additional cards, refresh the page (or scan the QR code again).

Once you enter The Ascent, draw cards by scanning the QR code for The Ascent.

When you near The Summit, draw cards by scanning the QR code for The Summit.

The first player to reach the top of Multidisciplinarity Mountain will scan the QR code to learn what lies on The Horizon.
